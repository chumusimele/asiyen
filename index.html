Three steps to cashless

Asiyen removes the friction of cash from your daily commute — no change, no hassle, no waiting.

01
📲

Download & top up

Download Asiyen, create your wallet and add money via EFT, card or an Asiyen voucher from your local agent.

02
📷

Scan the sticker

When you board your taxi, open Asiyen and scan the QR sticker on the seat or window. Enter your fare amount.

03
✅

Payment confirmed

Your wallet is debited instantly. The driver receives a notification. Both parties get a transaction record.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Asiyen Enterprise (Pty) Ltd ("Asiyen", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used for authentication via OTP
  • Full name — optional, used for personalisation
  • Transaction history — payments, top-ups, withdrawals, transfers
  • Device information — for security and fraud prevention

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via one-time password (OTP)
  • To process payments, transfers and withdrawals
  • To maintain your wallet balance and transaction records
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent fraud

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers located in the European Union (Ireland) with industry-standard encryption. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Payment processors (Peach Payments) — to process deposits and withdrawals
  • SMS providers (MessageBird) — to deliver OTP verification codes

Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by Asiyen
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to processing of your personal information

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as required by law. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 5 years in accordance with financial regulations.

Cookies

The Asiyen mobile app does not use cookies. This website uses minimal analytics to understand visitor behaviour — no personally identifiable information is collected.
